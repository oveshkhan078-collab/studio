# **App Name**: CampusCraft

## Core Features:

- 3D Game Design Sandbox: A sandbox environment for B.Tech students to create and design 3D puzzle games, encouraging creative exploration.
- AR/VR Engineering Visualizer: Augmented and Virtual Reality module to visualize complex machines and circuits, enhancing understanding and engagement.
- Tech News & Hackathon Alerts: A feed that delivers tech news and hackathon alerts, specifically tailored to events in Pune and online, ensuring students stay informed.
- AI Resume Assistant: Generative AI tool that gives personalized tips on resume improvement based on user profile and targeted internship roles.
- Smart Work/Study Companion: Includes Pomodoro timer, task tracking, and goal setting features to improve study and work habits.
- B.Tech Notes Marketplace: Platform for students to buy and sell peer-reviewed study materials, creating a collaborative learning environment.
- AI-Powered Doubt Solver: Uses AI to provide explanations for questions, offering solutions through diagrams or video links, catering to diverse learning preferences.
- Internship Finder: A tool that connects students with internship opportunities based on their profile and preferences.
- DY Patil Campus Connect: A module for accessing schedules, forums, and announcements specific to DY Patil campus.
- Hindi Calculator: An ad-integrated calculator with Hindi language support for quick calculations.
- AI Study Plan Generator: An AI tool to generate personalized study plans based on course load and exam dates.
- Coding Challenge Platform: A platform with coding challenges and competitions to enhance coding skills.
- Virtual Labs: Simulations of laboratory experiments for practical learning.
- Career Counseling Chatbot: A chatbot that offers career advice and guidance based on student's field of study.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant blue (#29ABE2) to evoke innovation and trust.
- Background color: A light gray (#F0F0F0) for a clean, modern look that reduces eye strain.
- Accent color: A bright green (#90EE90) to highlight interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif, provides a neutral and modern appearance suitable for all text.
- Use a consistent set of line icons to represent different sections and features, ensuring ease of navigation and visual appeal.
- Implement a modular design with clear separation between sections for enhanced user experience and content discoverability.
- Subtle animations to provide feedback and enhance interactivity without being distracting.
